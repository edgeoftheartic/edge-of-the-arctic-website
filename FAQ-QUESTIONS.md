# FAQ questions for Sarah & Nik to answer
*The contact page currently has 6 generic answers I drafted. These are the
questions guests actually ask before paying — especially for an $8k
expedition. Answer in your own voice (2–4 sentences each); I'll plug them in
with FAQ structured data so they can appear directly in Google results.
Skip any that don't apply — better fewer real answers than many vague ones.*

## Booking & money
1. How do I book, and when is my place confirmed?
2. What deposit do you take, and when is the balance due? *(site currently says: flagship $1,000, balance 120 days out — confirm)*
3. Are deposits refundable, and what is your cancellation policy — by trip type? *(the single most-read answer on any tour site; be precise)*
4. Can I pay in instalments? *(WeTravel payment plans — say yes and how)*
5. What currency do you charge in, and why do some trips show USD and others EUR/GBP?
6. What happens if YOU cancel a departure (weather, minimum numbers)?
7. Is my money protected? (Ferðamálastofa licence, how client funds are held)

## Before the trip
8. Do I need travel insurance, and what must it cover? *(esp. Greenland: medical evacuation)*
9. What visas/passports do travellers need for Iceland / Greenland (Denmark) / the Balkans?
10. How do I get to the starting point — which airport, and do you do pickups?
11. What should I pack — and what should I NOT bother packing? *(a beloved, shareable answer — be opinionated)*
12. How fit do I need to be, honestly? What's the hardest day on each trip type?
13. Can you accommodate dietary requirements / allergies? *(chef angle — this answer can sell)*
14. What's the single-traveller supplement / do solo travellers join easily?
15. Can children come? Age limits?

## On the trip
16. How many guests are really on a departure, and who guides it?
17. What's included in "all-inclusive" — and what's genuinely not?
18. Will I see the northern lights / whales / puffins — and what if we don't? *(honest answer builds more trust than a promise)*
19. What's the accommodation standard on each journey (hotels? the camp in Tasermiut? the gulet)?
20. Is there phone signal / wifi in Greenland and the remote northeast?
21. How much walking is optional vs required?
22. What's the weather really like on [Greenland in August / Iceland in winter / the Adriatic in April]?

## Greenland flagship specifics
23. What is the Amarok, and what are the cabins like?
24. How rough can the coastal voyage get — what about seasickness?
25. What is Tasermiut Camp — tents? beds? bathrooms? *(people fear the word "camp"; describe it well)*
26. Why only two departures a year?

## Stay & restaurant
27. Can I book a room in Hjalteyri without a tour? Rates?
28. Do I need to reserve at Eyri, and how far ahead in summer?

## Format request
For each answer: plain language, first person ("we"), no marketing fluff,
and where a number exists, give the number. If an answer differs by trip,
answer per trip in one or two lines each.
