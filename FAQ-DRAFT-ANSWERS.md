**11. What should I pack — and what should I not bother with?**

What you don't need: a formal wardrobe, an umbrella (the wind will win), or heavy hiking gear for our gentler trips. We'll send a tailored packing list for your specific journey and season.
*✅ Confident — standard, well-tested Iceland advice, and a genuinely shareable answer. Tweak to taste.*

## On the trip

**16. How many guests are on a departure, and who guides it?**

**17. What's included in "all-inclusive," and what genuinely isn't?**
> On our all-inclusive journeys, effectively everything on the ground is included: accommodation, most or all meals, transport, guiding, excursions and admissions. What's not included is international airfare to the starting point, travel insurance (required), alcoholic drinks, and personal spending. Each trip page lists exactly what's covered.
*✅ Confident — matches the flagship inclusions and your WeTravel "all-inclusive" descriptions.*

**18. Will I definitely see the northern lights, whales or puffins?**

But these are wild, natural phenomena and no honest operator can guarantee them. What we can promise is the local knowledge to maximise your odds, and a wonderful trip regardless of what nature does on the night.
