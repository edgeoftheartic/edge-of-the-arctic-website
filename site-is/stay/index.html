The place

The real Iceland, twenty minutes from town

Staying with us puts you at the centre of the north: Akureyri in twenty minutes, Goðafoss in under an hour, whale watching from the fjord, and the wild northeast coast within a day's drive.

Comfortable double and twin rooms with fjord or mountain views, a shared guest kitchen and lounge, and breakfast by arrangement. And dinner? Eyri Restaurant is a two-minute walk — guests staying with us always get a table.
