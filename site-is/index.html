Explore the Area
The village

A place most travellers drive past

Hjalteyri is a former herring station on the western shore of Eyjafjörður — a handful of houses, a historic factory turned art space, seals in the shallows and mountains across the water.

It's the kind of place people mean when they say "the real Iceland": quiet, beautiful, and twenty minutes from everything that matters in the north. We've made it our home — come and share it.
