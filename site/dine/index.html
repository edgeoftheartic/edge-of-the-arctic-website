Eyri Restaurant

"Traditional Icelandic cuisine elevated with a high-end twist" — SCAN Magazine. In an old fishing village on the fjord, twenty minutes north of Akureyri.

Reserve a Table Hours & Directions
The kitchen

Everything close to home

Eyri is chef Nik Peros's restaurant on the shore at Hjalteyri, where the menu is written by the season and the fjord. Fresh fish landed in the north, Icelandic lamb from farms within thirty kilometres, house-smoked specialties made the traditional way, and thoughtful vegetarian and vegan dishes.

Choose the three- or four-course seasonal tasting menu, or order à la carte — with curated Mediterranean wines and local craft beers alongside.

Eyri is also the heart of our expeditions: every Edge of the Arctic & Beyond journey through North Iceland finishes with an evening at this table.
