Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal data. Last updated July 2026.

Who we are

This website is operated by Langanes Ferðamannaþjónusta, trading as Edge of the Arctic & Beyond ("we", "us", "our"), Hjalteyrarvegur 11, 604 Hjalteyri, Iceland. We are the data controller for the personal information described in this policy.

Questions about privacy or your data? Email nik@edgeofthearctic.travel or call +354 862 9697.

What we collect

We only collect what we need to answer your enquiry and arrange your travel:

  • Contact details you give us (name, email, phone) when you submit an enquiry or booking form
  • Trip details you share (dates, party size, interests, dietary needs)
  • Booking and payment information, which is processed by our booking and payment partners — we do not store your card details
  • Basic, anonymous website analytics (see Cookies below)

How we use it

We use your information to reply to your enquiry, design and operate your journey, process bookings and payments through our partners, and — only if you ask us to — keep you informed about future trips. We do not sell your data to anyone.

Our lawful bases under GDPR are: your consent (when you contact us or opt in), and the performance of a contract (when you book a trip).

Who we share it with

To arrange your travel we share the minimum necessary information with trusted partners:

  • Our booking platform, WeTravel, for scheduled trips (payments, payment plans, participant details)
  • Stripe, for card payments where used — Stripe processes your card directly and we never see the full number
  • Web3Forms, which delivers website enquiry forms to our email inbox
  • Accommodations, guides, transport and activity providers on your itinerary, as needed to deliver your trip

Each of these partners maintains its own privacy policy and appropriate safeguards.

Cookies & analytics

This is a static website with no advertising trackers. We use only privacy-respecting, aggregate analytics to understand general traffic (for example, how many people visit a page). Embedded booking checkouts and maps may set their own cookies when you use them; those are governed by the relevant provider's policy.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry and booking records only as long as needed to serve you and to meet legal, accounting and tax obligations, after which they are securely deleted.

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to our use of your personal data, and to data portability. To exercise any of these, email nik@edgeofthearctic.travel and we'll respond promptly. You may also lodge a complaint with the Icelandic Data Protection Authority (Persónuvernd).

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; the current version always lives at this page with its "last updated" date.
