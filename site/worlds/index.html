Good to know

A championship above the 65th parallel — a little planning goes a long way.

🌡️ October weather

Cool and changeable: roughly 0–7 °C, wind and the first snow possible. Pack layers and waterproofs.

🌌 Northern lights

October nights are dark enough for aurora — one of the best reasons to add a day or two either side.

🚘 Getting around

Hjalteyri sits ~25 min north of Akureyri. A car or pre-booked transfer is the easiest way in.

💳 Practicalities

Currency is the Icelandic króna (ISK); cards work everywhere. Language: Icelandic, with English widely spoken.
