Your hosts

We live where you're going.

Edge of the Arctic & Beyond is Nik and Sarah — chef, guides and hosts. Not an agency reselling someone else's trips: the people who cook your dinner, drive the road north and meet you at the airport.

Our story

It started at the actual edge of the Arctic

Our story began in Þórshöfn, a fishing town on the Langanes peninsula in Iceland's remote northeast, where Nik opened a small guesthouse and restaurant. Guests kept asking the same question: "Can you show us around?"

Those drives along the northeast coast — to gannet cliffs, abandoned villages and the Arctic Henge — grew into a tour company. Today we're based at Hjalteyri on Eyjafjörður, where Nik cooks at our restaurant, Eyri, and together we lead small-group journeys through North Iceland and, now, Greenland.

Everything we offer passes one test: would we take our own friends there?

How we're different

Small, personal, and rooted here

Genuinely small groups

Our expeditions carry a maximum of 12 guests, and many journeys are private. You'll never look for your guide across a crowded coach park.

Chef-led food, everywhere

Nik is a chef first. Every journey eats the way our restaurant cooks: local, seasonal, and chosen personally — from harbour cafés to our own dining room.

We host you, start to finish

The same people plan your journey, answer your emails, guide your days and pour your last-night wine. That's the whole idea.
