Frequently Asked Questions

How do payments work?

Scheduled group trips are booked through our secure WeTravel pages (card or bank transfer, with payment plans available). Flagship expedition deposits can also be paid by card via Stripe. We never handle your card details ourselves.

What's your group size?

Twelve guests maximum on any journey — most run with fewer. Tailor-made trips can be for as few as two.

Are deposits refundable?

Flagship expedition deposits are fully refundable for 30 days. Each scheduled trip's terms are shown clearly on its booking page before you pay.

Do I need travel insurance?

Yes — comprehensive travel insurance including medical evacuation is required for Greenland expeditions, and strongly recommended for all journeys.

How fit do I need to be?

If you can walk comfortably for a couple of hours on uneven ground, you can join any of our journeys. Activities are optional — the pace is yours.

Can you handle dietary needs?

Happily — the chef is one of your hosts. Vegetarian, vegan and most dietary requirements are easy; just tell us when you book.
