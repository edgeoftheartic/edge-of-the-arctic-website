Why Slovenia, with us

Slovenia Tours

Slovenia is Europe's best-kept travel secret: an alpine lake with a church on an island, a capital that banned cars from its riverfront, karst caves the size of cathedrals and orange-wine country that sommeliers whisper about.

We pair it with Croatia on our spring journeys — the Adriatic route north — so you taste both countries' tables in one trip, with the driving handled and the restaurants chosen personally.

Prefer Slovenia on its own, at your pace? That's a tailor-made journey — tell us your dates.
