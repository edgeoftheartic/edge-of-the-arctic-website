Why Greece, with us

Greece with local hosts

Our Greece is not the packaged one. Chef-led and taverna-first, it follows the food: Crete's mountain villages and olive presses, markets with the fishermen still arguing, and wine made by families who never bothered with labels.

Past journeys — like Robin's Authentic Greece and Crete — were built privately around a group's appetite. That's the model: tell us who's coming and what you love to eat, and we design the route.

Greece journeys run as private and custom departures, typically in spring and autumn when the islands exhale.
