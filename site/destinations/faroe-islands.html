Why Faroe Islands, with us

Faroe Islands with local hosts

The Faroes are what happens when Norse village life meets some of the steepest sea cliffs in the world. Puffins nest above fjords, waterfalls fall straight into the ocean, and every valley hides a village that seems painted on.

Our six-day adventure moves at Faroese pace: proper time in Tórshavn, the postcard villages of Gásadalur and Saksun, boat trips under the bird cliffs, and food that leans hard on the sea.

Small group, personally hosted, and easily combined with time in Iceland on either side — ask us to build the connection.
