Why Bosnia & Herzegovina, with us

Bosnia & Herzegovina with local hosts

Cross the border from Croatia and the crowds simply stop. Bosnia & Herzegovina rewards the curious: Mostar's rebuilt bridge and its divers, Sarajevo where empires stacked their stories, Blagaj's dervish house under a cliff, and rivers the colour of glacier ice.

Our twelve-day journey weaves hidden Bosnia into coastal Croatia — the full arc from Adriatic islands to mountain monasteries — with food and people at the centre throughout.

A seven-day Bosnia-only version, starting in Sarajevo, is available on request.
