Why Bulgaria, with us

Bulgaria Tours

Bulgaria rarely makes the shortlist, which is exactly why it should: Rila Monastery's painted arcades, Plovdiv's Roman old town, the Valley of Roses in bloom, and mountains — Rila, Pirin, Rhodope — that would be famous anywhere else.

The food surprises everyone: shopska salads, slow lamb, mountain cheeses and a wine tradition five thousand years deep, at prices that feel like a misprint.

We run Bulgaria as private and custom journeys — often paired with the wider Balkans. Tell us your dates and appetite.
