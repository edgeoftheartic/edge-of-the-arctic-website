Why Iceland, with us

Iceland with local hosts

Most Iceland tours loop the same crowded ring of sights. Ours are built from the north — Akureyri, Eyjafjörður and the wild northeast coast — where the whales outnumber the coaches and the northern lights come without the queues.

Every journey is hosted personally, capped at twelve guests, and fed properly: Nik is a chef first, and every itinerary eats the way our restaurant at Hjalteyri cooks — local, seasonal, honest.

Come for a reset week on the fjord, an aurora hunt timed to the new moon, or the whole two-country flagship expedition.

What to experience

Iceland, distilled

Eyjafjörður & Akureyri

Whale watching on a calm fjord, geothermal baths, the Forest Lagoon and Iceland's northern capital — twenty minutes from our door.

The northeast coast

Dettifoss, Ásbyrgi and the Arctic Henge at Raufarhöfn — the Iceland most visitors never reach. We wrote a whole guide to it.

The dark-sky winter

From September to April the aurora dances over Hjalteyri. Our winter departures are timed to the new moon for the darkest skies.
