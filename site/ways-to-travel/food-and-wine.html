Journeys built around the table

With deep roots in the restaurant world and a passion for great food, creating culinary travel comes naturally to us. As chefs and hospitality professionals, we believe food, wine and local tradition are the truest way into a destination.

Our curated Food, Wine & Culture journeys currently reach Iceland, Greenland, Croatia, Greece, Slovenia, Bosnia, Bulgaria and the Faroe Islands — with more being added. From the seafood traditions of the North Atlantic to the rich flavours of Southern Europe and the Balkans, each is designed to taste, discover and connect.
