Why choose a custom adventure

Away from the buses, into the real North

Iceland is a land of magical landscapes, but the best of it is where most travellers never see. Based in Akureyri and the remote North-East, we offer a perspective only locals can — rugged highlands, hidden fjords and secret waterfalls, reached in a specialised 4×4.

For true adventurers, our Land Rover and jeep journeys provide the comfort and capability to tackle the island's most challenging terrain. We build every itinerary from scratch around your passion — whether that's fly fishing, birdwatching, photography or food.

This is luxury Arctic travel that never feels clinical: comfort, quality and exclusive small groups that feel like travelling with friends — flexible enough that if the aurora appears, we simply stay out.
