Occasionally, factors beyond our control — weather, safety, supplier changes, or a departure not reaching its minimum group size — require us to alter or cancel a trip. If we cancel, we will offer you an alternative date or a refund of monies paid to us for the affected arrangements. Travel insurance

Comprehensive travel insurance is a condition of joining our expeditions. For Greenland and remote journeys, your policy must include medical cover and emergency evacuation. We recommend cover for cancellation, curtailment, baggage and personal liability for all trips.

You are responsible for holding a valid passport and any visas required for your journey, and for meeting any health or entry requirements. We're happy to advise, but requirements can change and remain your responsibility to confirm.
