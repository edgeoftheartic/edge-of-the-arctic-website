Greenland by Sea & Arctic Iceland

A rare expedition through the world's largest island: Nuuk, the overnight Amarok coastal voyage, colourful Qaqortoq, the granite cathedral of Tasermiut Fjord — finished with three days of whale watching, geothermal baths and exceptional food in North Iceland.

Two departures. Twelve guests each. 24 places a year.
